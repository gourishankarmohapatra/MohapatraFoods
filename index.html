Our Heritage

Nurturing Health and Taste from Cuttack

At Mohapatra Foods Private Limited, we combine modern food technology with traditional craftsmanship to deliver products that represent quality, hygiene, and authenticity.

Pursuant to our Memorandum of Association (MOA), we engage in the complete business cycle of food production: from sourcing, cultivating, and processing, to freeze-drying, packing, and global export. Our facility is situated in Nuasahi, Bidanasi, Cuttack, an area rich in agricultural commerce.
